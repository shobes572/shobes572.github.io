Come one, Come all! Try your hand at this Javascript Quiz and see how you place amongs the other developers who have tried before you. All questions are multiple choice, you can answer your Number Keys, or press the [S] key to skip.
Scoring:

  1. Correctly answering a question adds two seconds.
  2. Incorrectly answering a question removes two seconds.
  3. Your Score is computed from the number of questions correctly answered when the timer hits zero.
  4. You can earn even more points if you reach the end of the quiz with time remaining.
